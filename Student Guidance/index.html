The End of your Search is here

Passing out of school is a big time for most students because for most it is the first time they will have to make a life-changing choice, their undergraduate degree. This Online Student Guidance System aims to be a helpful and useful starting point for this confusing phase. There is a wealth of tools and data available for students who are interested in Engineering field but most students do not have the time to research as well as required. This system will bring all these tools and data in one place for students. The Online Student Guidance System begins with choosing a career. Students will be able to see various career choices and the pros and cons of the same. They will be able to view company profiles and job experiences by people in the field. They will be able to view data from sites like Naukri.com and LinkedIn about professionals in each career. Different government sites that provide job statistics will also be linked with each career. All this information will help future college students to make a well – informed choice.

Why Engineering ?

In current times, engineering has offered a beacon of hope to aspiring students as a stable career choice. It’s a great way to future-proof your career and branch out from a technical standpoint. Fields like civil and electronics engineering have always been safe picks. Still, of late, new and contemporary fields like nanotechnology and genetic engineering are becoming prominent with every passing academic year. In that regard, there are two main career paths you can choose if you pick engineering. Every course you study in an engineering degree will have some connection to technical applications. There’s no substitute for the time spent in the many workshops and engineering labs as part of the course. It’s what gives you a leg up over the countless people opting for a B.Sc. degree in the same specialization.
